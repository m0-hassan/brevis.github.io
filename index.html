Background

01 - 01

Brevis uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), allowing us to finetune responses which allows for greater adaptability, security, and control over the data it processes. By developing its own LLM, Brevis can optimize its system for highly specialized tasks and domain-specific insights, ensuring the highest levels of performance and reliability.

Background

01 - 02

Transitioning to college, especially at an institution like UChicago, can be a challenge for first-year students. These challenges range from academic adjustments to forming essential connections for career development. These considerations underscore the importance of a system designed to provide guidance tailored to both the academic and personal aspects of student life.

Background

01 - 03

While pre-orientation programs, the college catalog, and the academic advisor service provide foundational support, there is still ample room to enrich the onboarding experience through more immediate, targeted, and personalized advice. By leveraging AI and emerging technologies, students can receive just-in-time information and direction that helps them make informed decisions about their academic pathways. Moreover, these tools can also reduce the wait times and resource limitations often associated with traditional advising channels, ensuring a more equitable experience for all students.

Background

01 - 04

Our project introduces a digital advisor application that leverages artificial intelligence to offer personalized guidance to incoming undergraduates. By harnessing cutting-edge algorithms and data analytics, this system can provide recommendations for course selections, extracurricular involvement, and essential campus resources. Ultimately, the goal is to empower students to navigate the complexities of their new environment with confidence, ensuring they have the support needed to thrive academically and professionally.

Background

01 - 05

This AI chatbot analyzes data pulled from several UChicago-specific resources, including the college catalog, Registered Student Organization (RSO) websites, course reviews, student surveys, and more. By compiling and interpreting this information, the chatbot can provide nuanced answers that directly address the user's unique questions and challenges. The system’s capacity to learn from user interactions also allows it to refine its responses over time, growing increasingly adept at offering the most relevant guidance possible.

Background

01 - 06

Students will interact with this advisor through an intuitive interface accessible via web and mobile platforms, ensuring they receive context-aware support. This approach not only aids in smoothing their transition but also helps in laying a robust foundation for their academic and professional careers.

MOTIVATION

We wanted to build Brevis because we feel the key to success is having easy access to knowledge and resources critical for your success academically and profesionally.

LAST YEAR

We utilized Google Gemini API, but we ran into a multitude of issues of creating consistent responses in line with our goals with Brevis. We opted to utilize RAG for the chatbot and kept Google Gemini to generate academic schedules.

FRONT END

Part of creating an excellent application is presenting it in a clean and aesthetic manner, this time around we focused heavily on presentation using classic HTML & Javascript rather than React.

RAG

For better, more consistent responses we used Retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), a a technique for enhancing the accuracy and reliability of generative AI models with facts fetched from external sources.
